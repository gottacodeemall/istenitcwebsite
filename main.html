ISTE NITC STUDENTS' CHAPTER

It is commonly known that the forte of any engineering college is its depth in its technical acumen. In NITC, ISTE is one such organization that is the sole contributor to this cause. Controlled from its Headquarters in Delhi, ISTE is linked in all colleges throughout India including the premier IITs

Spectrum

Spectrum is an inter branch event conducted exclusively for the first year students of NITC. ISTE NITC Student Chapter through the past series of this event successfully inculcated a sense of camaraderie, team spirit, leadership and branch spirit among the first years.

Abdul Kalam Scholarship

Abdul Kalam Scholarship(AKS) The Abdul Kalam Scholarship(AKS) is the premier scholarship examination conducted by ISTE exclusively for the first years. Format: The AKS is divided into three stages based on which the judgement is made- Written test, Group Discussion (GD) and a Personal Interview.


Industrial Visit

Industrial visit is one of the primary events of ISTE NITC Students Chapter.It is organized every year with great concern.Industrial visit provide an insight of on the internal working environment of the company.


Career Workshop

Career Workshop will help you open up the various avenues in life after B.Tech. From preparing for various entrance examinations to the placement scenario in our college, career workshop is a must for all.
